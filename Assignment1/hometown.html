Vinh Long City

Vinh Long city which is the capital of Vinh Long Province, which branches out from the Mekong River at the narrows of My Thuan about 10 kilometres (6.2 mi) upstream, only to meet it later downstream.

My Thuan Bridge

My Thuan Bridge is a cable-stayed bridge over the Mekong river. Also, it is the largest overseas assistance project undertaken by the Australian government costing A$91 million.

Nguyen Binh Khiem Highschool for the Gifted

Founded in 1992, Nguyen Binh Khiem high school is located in Vinh Long City which is famous for its traditional fondness for learning. The school is specialized for gifted students who show their talents in academic fields such as natural science, social science as well as foreign languages.
